About

At Blooms, we believe that every bloom tells a story and every bouquet sets a mood. Founded with the vision of connecting people to the beauty of nature, Floom serves as a bridge between local artisan florists and discerning customers who seek unique, fresh, and captivating floral arrangements. Our commitment is to curate an exceptional range of handcrafted bouquets, crafted with passion and designed to inspire. By partnering with the best local talent, we ensure that every bouquet tells its own story, embodying the essence of its creator and the place from which it originates. With Floom, you're not just sending flowers; you're sharing a piece of the artisan's heart, the fragrance of a locale, and the spirit of a memory waiting to be created.A flower bouquet is a collection of flowers in a creative arrangement. Flower bouquets can be arranged for the decor of homes or public buildings, or may be handheld. Handheld bouquets are classified by several different popular shapes and styles, including nosegay, crescent, and cascading bouquets. Flower bouquets are often given for special occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries or funerals. They are also used extensively in weddings as well as Olympics Medal Ceremonies. Bouquets arranged in vases or planters for home decor can be arranged in either traditional or modern styles. Symbolism may be attached to the types of flowers used, according to the culture.
